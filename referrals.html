Fair Play & Abuse Prevention Notice

To ensure organic community building and prevent game manipulation, Together We Solve employs extensive verification parameters. Self-referrals, alternate/duplicate accounts, temporary or disposable email addresses, browser signature manipulation, and co-registration clusters are automatically audited. Referral points and experience weights are only credited upon successful validation by the Steward Council, and any progression values associated with invalid registrations will be retroactively revoked.
